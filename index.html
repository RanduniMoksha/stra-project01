Stars are glowing balls of hot gases that light up our night sky. They are born in giant clouds of dust and gas, live for millions or even billions of years, and end their lives in amazing ways. From tiny red dwarfs to massive supergiants, stars come in many sizes, colors, and types. They have guided travelers, inspired stories, and helped shape the universe we see today.
